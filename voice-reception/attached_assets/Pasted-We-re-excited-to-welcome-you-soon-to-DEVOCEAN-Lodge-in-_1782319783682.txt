Meet Your Host – Sean
Sean, your host, will be available throughout your stay. He's a treasure chest of local stories and insights — from daily village life to hidden gems, local markets, and unforgettable excursions. Don't be shy to ask; you might just walk away with a great story or two!

 
Meet the Lodge Family
Around the lodge, you might meet a cheerful young boy — that's Lennon, Sean's son. He recently got a sister too. Her name is Sienna. He's proud of his bunnies and often offers guests a little farm tour. What started as a small COVID-era project has blossomed into a mini-farm with rabbits and chickens, supporting food security for guests and staff alike.

Lisa, Lennon's mum, also offers wonderfully relaxing massages — highly recommended!

Directions to DEVOCEAN Lodge
Enter the village via the new tar road. You'll pass the local market ("barracas") on your left and the minibus taxi rank on your right.

Continue up the small hill until you see Love Café on your right.

Turn right immediately after the café and continue for about 100 metres.

When you spot the Police Station on your right, look for the DEVOCEAN sign and the wooden gate — feel free to open it and park inside.
