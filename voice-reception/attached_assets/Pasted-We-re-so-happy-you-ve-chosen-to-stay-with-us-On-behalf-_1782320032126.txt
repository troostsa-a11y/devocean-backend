🌴 Explore Ponta do Ouro
Beaches & Walks

Old Lighthouse Hike
Climb the highest hill near the South African border forest for breathtaking views over the bay. Afterwards, stop by Lua do Mar Restaurant — it's a great spot to watch dolphins year-round, whales from May to October, and turtles from December to January.
Tip: Take the trail around the point during high tide (about 3 hours before and after). Walking over the beach around the rocks would be risky around that time due to unexpected higher waves. Tide information is provided through the Tides4fishing link at the bottom.

Walk to Ponta Malongane
A scenic 8 km beach walk takes you to the neighbouring village. Climb through the banana trees to Campismo Nino for another panoramic view, then continue to Sky Island, home to Ponta Paragliding. You can return along the beach or follow the sandy 4×4 road back. Most of the time you'll be offered a lift along the way.
