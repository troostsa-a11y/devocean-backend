Kosi Bay / Ponta do Ouro Border Post Crossing
Crossing in Mozambique or South Africa
Border Opening Times:	08:00 - 17:00 / 7 days a week
Location:	KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa entering Mozambique, Maputo Province (20 min from Ponta do Ouro)
Fuel Stations:	There is a fuel station en-route in Manguzi (South Africa) and Ponta do Ouro (Mozambique)
Road Conditions:	Great depending on the route you plan to take however and please note: A 4x4 is required to reach Ponta Malongane, Ponta Mamoli, Santa Maria and various other locations from this border. The road to Ponta do Ouro village is now tarmacked. See below for more detailed information.

Opening times are from 08:00 - 17:00 / 7 days a week. Be at the border early because during the busy holiday period, there can be a large queue of cars waiting to get through the border. Take this into account when timing your travel to Ponta do Ouro and DO NOT BE LATE - the border closes at 5pm on the hour!

The wait at the border may take several hours so patience is key and do not act aggressive with any of the border officials.

Note: You are NOT allowed to bring any amount of beer into Mozambique (and don't chance it)! See Mozambique Duty Free Custom & Import Restrictions for more information.

Travelling without a 4x4?
Note: You do not need a 4x4 if you are travelling to Maputo (unlike Ponta Malongane & Ponta Mamoli). The road leading onto the N200 from the border going north is tarmacked all the way.

There is secure overnight parking areas on the South African side of the border for regular 2WD vehicles - please contact them (details below - or the border direct for more information) for daily parking fees (you're looking around R50 - R60 per night). 4x4 transfer services from the border to DEVOCEAN Lodge is offered (see below).
