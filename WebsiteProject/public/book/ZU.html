Yini inqubomgomo yenu yokukhansela? ▼

Inqubomgomo Yethu Yokukhansela
Ukuze sigcine izinto zilingana kuzo zonke izivakashi futhi siqinisekise ukuthi singalungiselela ukuhlala kwakho ngabasebenzi abafanele nezinto ezidingekayo, sisebenzisa inqubomgomo yokukhansela eyi-semi-flexible kuzo zonke ukubhukha okujwayelekile (ngaphandle kwezinye ezingabuyiselwa nemali nezokugcina zemizuzu).

  • Idiphozithi engu-50% ngesikhathi sokubhukha, nemali esele khokhwa ekufikeni.
  • Ukubuyiswa kwemali ngokugcwele kokukhansela okwenziwa ezinsukwini ezingu-28 noma ngaphezulu ngaphambi kokufika.
  • Ukukhansela ezinsukwini ezingu-28 zokufika kuhlelwa imali engu-50%.
  • Ukungafiki noma izinguquko ezifinyeza isikhathi kukhokhelwa u-100% wemali yonke.

Sithemba ukuthi lokhu kunikeza umhlahlandlela ocacile ngenkathi sinikeza ukuguquguquka okuningi ngangokunokwenzeka.

Yini inqubomgomo yenu yezingane? ▼

Inqubomgomo Yethu Yezingane

0–3 iminyaka (Izinsane)
Usana olulodwa noma umntwana omncane angahlala mahhala. Akudingeki uwufake esibalanikweni — vele ungeze inothi efomini lakho lokubhukha. Umbhede wosana ungahlelwa uma uyacelwa.

4–11 iminyaka (Izingane)
Izingane zihlala ngengxenye yentengo yabadala. Uma kudingeka, sinikeza umbhede owengeziwe we-Safari nomatilasi ukuze zihlale kahle.

12+ iminyaka (Izivakashi Ezincane)
Kusukela eminyakeni engu-12, intsha ibalwa njengabadala kokubili ekuhleleni nasezintengo. Sicela uwengeze esibalanikweni sabadala uma uqeda ukubhukha kwakho.

Ake nivumele ukuziphakela? ▼

Njengendawo yokuhlala enikezwa ukudla okugcwele, ngokuvamile asinikezi ukuziphakela. Nokho, ezivakashini ezihlala ubusuku obune noma ngaphezulu, kungenzeka ukuthi kuhleleleke ukusetshenziswa okulinganiselwe kwekhishi lendawo yokudlela ngqo neqembu lethu.

Ngaphezu kwesevisi yethu yendawo yokudlela, ungakwazi futhi ukujabulela:

  • I-braai yangasese ephahleni elungiselelwe wena
  • Izinketho ezinambithekayo zokudla kwasolwandle, njengomisila yezinkolela namasheleni
  • Ngaphezu kwezindawo zokudlela ezingu-15 nezincane ebangeni lokuhamba ngezinyawo

Isincomo esikhethekile yi-makhethee wendawo, lapho amalungu omphakathi anobungane angakulungisela ukudla kwendabuko — okuhlangenwe nakho okuhlukile okusekela ngqo imindeni yendawo.
