Frequently Asked Questions

What is your cancellation policy? ▼

Our Cancellation Policy
To keep things fair for all guests and to ensure we can prepare your stay with the right staff and supplies, we use a semi-flexible cancellation policy for all standard bookings (except non-refundable and last-minute promotions).

  • 50% deposit at booking, with the remaining balance due on arrival.
  • Full refund for cancellations made 28 days or more before arrival.
  • Cancellations within 28 days of arrival carry a 50% fee.
  • No-shows or changes that shorten the stay are charged at 100% of the total.

We hope this provides clear guidance while offering as much flexibility as possible.

Do you offer airport transfers? ▼

Yes — we provide a few reliable options to make your journey to DEVOCEAN as smooth as possible:

1. Meet & Greet with Minibus Connection
A driver will welcome you at the airport, accompany you to the downtown collectivo/chapa station, and make sure you are introduced to the station manager for your seat assignment.
It's not the most comfortable trip, but many guests enjoy it as a unique and authentic Mozambican travel experience.

  • Arrival before 15:00 required
  • Mt 1000 for meet-and-greet + taxi transfer (shareable)
  • Minibus cost: Mt 200 per person + Mt 100 per extra bag

2. Private Taxi Direct to the Lodge
A convenient door-to-door option for families or guests who prefer comfort.

  • USD 120 per one-way (up to 3 adults or 2 adults + 2 children)

3. Self-Transfer (DIY)
If you want full flexibility, email us and we'll send step-by-step directions and recommendations.

What is your child policy? ▼

Our Child Policy

0–3 years (Infants)
One baby or infant may stay free of charge. You don't need to include them in the headcount — simply add a note in your booking form. A baby cot can be arranged upon request.

4–11 years (Children)
Children stay at half the adult rate. If needed, we provide an additional Safari bed and mattress to make them comfortable.

12+ years (Young Guests)
From age 12, youngsters are counted as adults for both occupancy and pricing. Please add them to the adult number when completing your reservation.

Do you allow self-catering? ▼

As a fully catered lodge, we generally do not offer self-catering. However, for guests staying four nights or longer, it may be possible to arrange limited use of the restaurant kitchen directly with our team.

In addition to our own restaurant service, you can also enjoy:

  • A private rooftop barbecue (braai) prepared for you
  • Delicious seafood options, such as lobster tails and prawns
  • More than 15 restaurants and small eateries within walking distance

A special recommendation is the local market, where friendly community members can prepare traditional meals for you — a unique experience that directly supports local families.

Do I need a 4x4 to get to the Lodge? ▼

Reaching DEVOCEAN Lodge is easy — the access road is mostly tarred, and our parking is only 100 meters from the end of the new tar road, suitable for any normal vehicle or motorbike.

For exploring beyond the lodge, especially around the village or the neighboring Pontas, the roads become sandy. For this kind of adventure, a 4x4 is definitely recommended.
