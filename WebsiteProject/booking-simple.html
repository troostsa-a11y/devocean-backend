Frequently Asked Questions

What is your cancellation policy? ▼

Our Cancellation Policy
We offer a semi-flexible policy for all rates except non-refundable and last-minute offers.

A 50% deposit is required at the time of reservation; the remaining balance is due on arrival.

Free cancellation up to 28 days before arrival (full refund of the deposit).

Cancellations made 28 to 1 day before arrival incur a 50% fee.

No-shows or shortened stays are charged at 100% of the booking value.

We aim to keep our policy fair and transparent while ensuring we can guarantee availability and prepare your stay with care.

Do you offer airport transportation? ▼

Yes, we provide complimentary airport shuttle service for all guests staying three nights or longer. For shorter stays, transportation can be arranged for a nominal fee.

What is your child policy? ▼

Children under 3 stay free. Children 4-11 years pay half price. Youngsters 12 and up count as adults. Please note maximum occupancy limits per unit.

What dining options are available? ▼

We feature our signature ocean-view restaurant, a casual beachside grill, and a pool bar serving light fare and cocktails. Room service is also available.
