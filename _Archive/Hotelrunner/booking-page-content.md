## Room Types & Descriptions

### 1. Safari Tent - Shared Bathroom
**Image URL:** https://cdn-cms2.hotelrunner.com/assets/photos/large/5c9c1be0-5891-496f-a2c8-74efd59e85d8.jpg

**Description:**
Experience the perfect balance of **comfort and adventure** in our **Standard Safari Tent**. Set on a **spacious three by six meter platform**, this **twelve square meter canvas tent** is protected by a **side and back wall**, ensuring privacy and shelter while keeping you connected to nature.

Inside, you'll find **two single beds with pedestals and shaded lamps**, which can be combined into a **King-size bed** for who prefers the double bed configuration. Convenient **power points** allow for charging devices, and a **strong fan** ensures refreshing air circulation during hot summer nights. A **small shelving** unit provides space for clothing and essentials. The tent is equipped with mosquito mesh on all windows and doors for a peaceful night's sleep.

Step onto your **private terrace**, furnished with **rolled palm-leaf chairs**, where you can relax and take in the view of the **lush tropical garden**, beautifully illuminated at night for a romantic ambiance.

Guests have access to **shared bathrooms**, separate for **ladies and gents**, each equipped with **two hot/cold water showers, two toilets and cold water sinks**.

Please note that during **public holiday weekends** and the **peak holiday season** (late December to early January), the surrounding village may be lively. Complimentary **earplugs** are available at reception for a restful night's sleep.

The **Standard Safari Tent** offers a **spacious, nature-immersed stay with modern comforts**, perfect for those looking to experience the outdoors without sacrificing convenience.

### 2. Comfort Tent - Private Bathroom
**Image URL:** https://cdn-cms1.hotelrunner.com/assets/photos/large/bafaeaa4-a3d3-4f07-ae0f-2ec64c74897a.jpg

**Description:**
For those seeking a **nature-immersed stay with extra privacy and comfort**, the **Comfort Safari Tent** offers an upgraded safari experience. This **spacious twelve square meter tent** is set on **its own platform**, featuring **two side walls and a back wall** for enhanced seclusion compared to the Standard Safari Tent.

Inside, you'll find **two single beds with pedestals and shaded lamps**, which can be combined into a **King-size bed**. **Power points** are available for charging devices, while a **strong fan** ensures air circulation during warm summer nights. The tent is equipped with **mosquito mesh** on all windows and doors for a peaceful night's sleep. **Lighting is conveniently controlled** by switches located near the entrance.

The **private wooden terrace**, furnished with **rolled palm-leaf chairs**, provides a perfect spot to unwind while overlooking the **lush tropical garden**, romantically illuminated at night. A **small shelving unit** offers storage for clothing and essentials.

At the back of the tent, under a **charming grass-thatched roof**, you'll find your **private bathroom**, complete with a **shower, toilet, and sink**—offering both convenience and a unique outdoor touch.

While Devocean Lodge is a peaceful retreat, the **surrounding village** can be lively during **public holiday weekends and the peak holiday season** (late December to early January). Complimentary **earplugs** are available at reception to ensure a restful stay.

The **Comfort Safari Tent** is the perfect choice for guests looking for a **spacious, well-appointed, and private safari-style getaway** with the convenience of an **en-suite bathroom**.

### 3. AC Garden Cottage
**Image URL:** https://cdn-cms1.hotelrunner.com/assets/photos/large/8c13e90f-11c2-4da1-99c5-83a9ef4495b0.jpg

**Description:**
Nestled within our **lush tropical garden**, the **Garden Cottage** at Devocean Lodge blends comfort with rustic elegance. Its **red Roman-tiled roof** and **high white ceiling**, beautifully compartmented with **dark brown wooden beams**, create a spacious and airy retreat. The **terracotta-tiled floor** adds warmth, complementing the cottage's inviting atmosphere.

Step outside onto your **private wooden terrace**, furnished with **camping chairs and a side table**, where you can unwind while enjoying the surrounding greenery. Inside, the soft **wheatfields-painted walls** enhance the serene ambiance.

Designed for both relaxation and functionality, the cottage features a **Queen-size boxed bed**, a **working desk with a shaded lamp**, and a **dinner table with chairs**. Thoughtful storage solutions include **suitcase shelves, a clothes rack, and additional shelving.**

For year-round comfort, the **inverter air conditioning** provides both cooling and heating. The **private bathroom**, housed in a charming **grass-thatched roundavel**, offers a **shower, toilet, and sink** for a unique yet modern touch.

Soft lighting options, including a **dimmable main light**, allow you to set the perfect mood for any occasion. Whether you're here to work, rest, or explore, the **Garden Cottage** offers a **cozy, and tranquil hideaway**.
