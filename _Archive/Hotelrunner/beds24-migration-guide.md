# Beds24 Setup Guide - DEVOCEAN Lodge Style Match

## Overview
This guide helps you set up Beds24 booking to match the DEVOCEAN Lodge website style at https://devoceanlodge.com

## Design Characteristics to Match

### Color Palette (from website)
- **Ocean Blue:** Primary branding color
- **Warm Sand:** Accent color
- **Deep Teal:** Secondary color

- **Clean whites and subtle grays for backgrounds**

### Typography
- **Font Family:** Inter (modern, clean, professional)
- **Headings:** Bold, clear hierarchy
- **Body text:** Readable, comfortable spacing

### Design Style
- **Card-based layouts** with subtle shadows
- **Image-first approach** - large, high-quality photos

- **Clean, uncluttered interface**

### Room 1: Safari Tent - Shared Bathroom

**Room Name:** Safari Tent - Shared Bathroom

**Short Description (for cards):**
12 m² canvas tent on a 3×6 m platform. Twin/King, fan, power points, mosquito mesh, private terrace. Shared ablutions.

**Full Description:**
Experience the perfect balance of comfort and adventure in our Standard Safari Tent. Set on a spacious three by six meter platform, this twelve square meter canvas tent is protected by a side and back wall, ensuring privacy and shelter while keeping you connected to nature.

Inside, you'll find two single beds with pedestals and shaded lamps, which can be combined into a King-size bed for who prefers the double bed configuration. Convenient power points allow for charging devices, and a strong fan ensures refreshing air circulation during hot summer nights. A small shelving unit provides space for clothing and essentials. The tent is equipped with mosquito mesh on all windows and doors for a peaceful night's sleep.

Step onto your private terrace, furnished with rolled palm-leaf chairs, where you can relax and take in the view of the lush tropical garden, beautifully illuminated at night for a romantic ambiance.

Guests have access to shared bathrooms, separate for ladies and gents, each equipped with two hot/cold water showers, two toilets and cold water sinks.

Please note that during public holiday weekends and the peak holiday season (late December to early January), the surrounding village may be lively. Complimentary earplugs are available at reception for a restful night's sleep.

The Standard Safari Tent offers a spacious, nature-immersed stay with modern comforts, perfect for those looking to experience the outdoors without sacrificing convenience.

**Amenities/Features:**
- Two single beds (or King) with pedestals and shaded lamps
- Mosquito mesh on doors/windows
- Strong fan
- Power points
- Small shelving unit for clothing/essentials
- Private terrace with rolled palm-leaf chairs facing tropical garden
- Shared bathrooms (ladies/gents): 2 hot/cold showers & 2 toilets each
- Village can be lively during holidays; free earplugs at reception

The Comfort Safari Tent is the perfect choice for guests looking for a spacious, well-appointed, and private safari-style getaway with the convenience of an en-suite bathroom.

## Beds24 Configuration Recommendations

### 1. **Visual Settings**
- **Theme/Colors:** Match Ocean Blue (#0EA5E9 or similar), Warm Sand accents
- **Font:** Choose clean, modern sans-serif (similar to Inter)
- **Layout:** Card-based room display with large images
- **Button Style:** Rounded corners, Ocean Blue background with white text

### 3. **Multi-Currency Support**
Configure these currencies based on your CC_TO_CURRENCY mapping:
- USD (United States, Default)
- EUR (Europe)
- GBP (United Kingdom)
- ZAR (South Africa, Namibia, Lesotho, Eswatini)
- JPY (Japan)
- CNY (China)
- RUB (Russia)
- TRY (Turkey)
