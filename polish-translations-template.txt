19. Headline: What to do in Ponta do Ouro
    POLISH: 

20. Note: For diving, snorkel trips, dolphin/whale seafaris and fishing charters, see the providers below.
    POLISH: 

--- Activity 1 ---
21. Title: Lighthouse hike & Lua Do Mar
    POLISH: 

22. Body: Hike to the Old Lighthouse for panoramic views. Continue to Lua Do Mar Restaurant on the dune. Often spot dolphins year-round, whales (May–Oct) and turtles (Dec–Jan). Mind the tide: avoid the beach route from 3 hours before to 3 hours after high tide; rogue waves can push you against rocks.
    POLISH: 

--- Activity 2 ---
23. Title: Walk to Ponta Malongane
    POLISH: 

24. Body: Beach-walk along the shore to the next village. Midway, stairs through banana trees lead to Campismo Nino Pub & Restaurant with great views. Near the bay transition, a path to Sky Island offers paragliding. Famous pubs in Malongane: Drunken Clam and Sunset Shack. Return via beach or the sandy 4×4 road.
    POLISH: 

--- Activity 3 ---
25. Title: Surf, dive & ocean research
    POLISH: 

26. Body: Surfboard rentals at the Beach Bar; several dive centers in the village and a dolphin/whale research center.
    POLISH:

27. Title: Eat & unwind nearby

28. Body: There are ~16 pubs and restaurants within 500 m of DEVOCEAN. For a relaxing full-body massage, ask for Lisa (LIZ-Way Massage).
